About The Company

MENA LIMITED is a leading HR Consultancy & Placement company with 40+ years of experience. Headquartered in Mumbai, we have branch offices across major Indian cities. Our services cover end-to-end recruitment and workforce management solutions. We connect organizations with skilled professionals across various industries.

We also operate internationally in the Gulf, South Asia, and Africa regions. Our overseas offices include KSA, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, and more. We source talent from Nepal, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Pakistan, and the Philippines. This global reach enables us to provide a truly diverse and qualified workforce.

Our team is led by Ms. Nancy R, an MBA in International Business Management from the United States of America, and a certified SAP-HCM Consultant (Germany). MENA Limited has a team of HR, IT and Management Professionals engaged in Executive Search and Executive Coaching.

Our team of consultants has a “never say die” attitude. They understand the dynamic, fast-changing environment and are committed to finding the best-suited talent for our clients, and the best job-fit for the prospects.

At MENA LIMITED, we provide a comprehensive range of reliable and efficient HR and facility management services, including manpower consultancy. Our expertise spans across various industries, offering tailored solutions to meet unique organizational needs.
